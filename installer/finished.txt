eXist has been installed into directory $INSTALL_PATH.

On Windows, click on the "eXist Database Startup" icon (if desktop 
shortcuts have been installed) or choose from the start menu. 

To start the database manually, call

$INSTALL_PATH/bin/startup.sh

or

$INSTALL_PATH/bin/startup.bat

Then browse to

http://localhost:8080/exist/index.xml

and read the quickstart guide.
